How to Play

  • Red moves first - Tap on a piece to select it
  • Regular pieces move diagonally forward one square
  • Kings can move any distance diagonally (like a bishop in chess)
  • Jump over opponent pieces to capture them
  • When a piece reaches the opposite end, it becomes a King
